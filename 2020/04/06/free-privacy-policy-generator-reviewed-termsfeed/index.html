Free Privacy Policy Generator Reviewed: TermsFeed

Published: April 6, 2020 • Free Templates, ToU & Privacy

My evaluation of the TermsFeed free Privacy Policy generator. Sample policy generated here, using only the free options.

I.                   REGULATIONS ADDRESSED
 
       YES
       NO
– COPPA (Children’s privacy)
– Cookies
– Advertising
 
–  GDPR (EU users)
–  CCPA (California users)
–  CalOPPA (DoNotTrack disclosure)
–  Direct marketing
II.                LOOK AND FEEL:
 
·         Preview without having to fill in fields or give email? No. Must fill in fields and give email. 
·         Formats offered:
o    DOCX
o    HTML
o    PDF 
o    Copy text to clipboard
o    They’ll host unique URL with your generated PP
·         Allowed to host PP on your own platform?  Yes
·         PP includes the generator author’s links/acknowledgements? No
·         Excessive ads/popups? No
 
III.             CONTENT:
·         Claim to be written by a lawyer? No
·         Appears professionally-written?  Yes
·         Plain English or legalese? Balanced
·         Explanations/instructions/help/hints?  No
·         Level of customization? None – LOW– Adequate – Too Much. Must pay for legally required provisions.
·         Right to modify at any time? Yes
·         Can be used for both website and app? No, website only. Must pay to add app.
·         Addresses cases of selling of PI? No
·         Newsletter option? No
·         Security issues disclaimer? Yes
·         Effective Date? Yes
·         Contact Us field?  Yes
 
 
IV.              VERDICT: 2/10
 
The free version is inadequate. Does not address the most important privacy regulations.
