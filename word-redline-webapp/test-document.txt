SAMPLE CONTRACT FOR TESTING

This is a sample contract between the Company and the Consultant.

1. SERVICES
The Consultant shall provide consulting services to the Company within 60 days of the effective date.

2. COMPENSATION
The Company shall pay the Consultant a fee of $5,000 for the services.

3. TERM
This Agreement shall commence on the effective date and continue for a period of 60 days.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Consultant acknowledges that they may receive confidential information from the Company.

5. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

END OF DOCUMENT
