Long-Term Visa Options

Thailand offers several pathways for foreigners seeking extended stays beyond tourist visas

✈

DTV: Digital Nomad Visa

5-year multiple entry for remote workers

  • 180 days per entry, extendable New 2024
  • 500,000 THB savings requirement
  • Remote work for foreign employers allowed
  • Dependents (spouse + children) eligible
  • No work permit needed for remote work
🏅

Soft Power Visa Categories

DTV via Muay Thai, cooking, meditation

  • Muay Thai training at certified gyms Popular
  • Thai cooking courses
  • Buddhism and meditation retreats
  • Medical treatment and wellness
  • Sports, music festivals, seminars
🏠

LTR: 10-Year Resident Visa

For high earners and investors

  • 4 categories: Wealthy, Pensioner, WFT, Skilled Premium
  • 17% flat tax for Highly-Skilled category
  • Work permit included
  • Annual reporting (not 90-day)
  • Fast-track airport immigration
⭐

Thailand Elite (Privilege)

Premium membership for hassle-free stays

  • 5-20 year membership options Premium
  • VIP airport services and lounges
  • No income requirements
  • Personal concierge services
  • Multiple tier options
👴

Retirement Visa (O-A / O-X)

Long-stay options for retirees 50+

  • O-A: 1-year, renewable Popular
  • O-X: 10-year premium option
  • 800,000 THB bank requirement
  • Health insurance mandatory
  • 90-day reporting required
💍

Marriage Visa Extension

Stay based on Thai spouse

  • 1-year extensions available
  • 400,000 THB financial requirement
  • Marriage registration required
  • Home visit inspections
  • Work permit eligible
