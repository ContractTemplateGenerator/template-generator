Thailand Cannabis & CBD Laws

Comprehensive guide to Thailand's cannabis regulations after the June 2025 re-criminalization. Medical access, CBD rules, business licensing, and what foreigners need to know.

Critical: June 2025 Regulatory Change

Recreational cannabis was re-criminalized in Thailand on June 26, 2025. Cannabis flowers are now classified as a "controlled herb." Only medical use with a valid Thai prescription remains legal. Many tourists are unaware of this change - penalties include fines up to 25,000 THB and imprisonment.
