‚ö†Ô∏è Why Cannabis Needs Its Own Pillar

Thailand's cannabis laws have undergone dramatic changes in 2024-2025:

  • 2022: Cannabis delisted from narcotics - recreational use tolerated
  • 2024: Push to re-regulate, restrict to medical use only
  • 2025: New Cannabis Act pending - recreational ban expected
  • Foreigners are particularly vulnerable during this transition period
