Power of Attorney General

Authorize someone to act on your behalf for financial, legal, or specific matters

Type of Power of Attorney
Grants broad authority for all financial and legal matters

Note: This is a general/financial POA. For healthcare decisions, use a Healthcare Power of Attorney or Advance Directive.

Principal (Person Granting Power)
Agent (Attorney-in-Fact)
Successor Agent (Optional)

Powers Granted

Duration & Termination
Specify when this POA becomes effective

Important: A general POA terminates upon the Principal's incapacity. Choose "Durable" if you want the POA to continue if you become incapacitated.
