Understanding Referral Agreements

A referral agreement provides for payment of commission for referred business. It establishes the terms under which a referral partner will introduce prospective clients to a company in exchange for compensation.

Key Components:

  • Commission Structure: Percentage of sale, flat fee, or recurring payments
  • Qualifying Referrals: Clear criteria for what constitutes an eligible referral
  • Payment Terms: When and how commissions are paid
  • Non-Circumvention: Protection against bypassing the referral relationship
  • Non-Solicitation: Preventing poaching of employees or consultants
  • Term and Termination: Duration and exit provisions
