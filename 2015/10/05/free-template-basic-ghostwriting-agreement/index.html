Why Use a Ghostwriting Agreement?

A ghostwriting agreement protects both the author and the writer by clearly establishing ownership, confidentiality, and payment terms. Key benefits:

  • IP ownership clarity - Ensures the author owns all rights to the work
  • Confidentiality protection - Keeps the ghostwriting arrangement private
  • Payment security - Defines compensation and payment schedule
  • Plagiarism warranty - Protects against copyright infringement claims
  • Clear deliverables - Specifies what will be delivered and when

Common Ghostwriting Arrangements

  • Books & Memoirs - Full-length manuscripts, often with milestone payments
  • Blog Posts & Articles - Ongoing content creation, often per-word or per-piece
  • E-books - Digital publications, typically flat fee arrangements
  • Speeches - One-time content for presentations or events
