Risk factors Stripe watches

Use this tab to match Stripe’s risk models with your own counter-evidence so the demand letter anticipates their defenses.

Volume spikes

Going from $500/day to $15K/day flags laundering concerns. Document launches, promos, or seasonal spikes.

Cross-border mix

Heavy international volume raises fraud risk. Keep KYC logs, IP records, and geolocation data.

Fulfillment delays

Extended shipping or unclear timelines correlate with disputes. Provide tracking data and fulfillment SOPs.

Quality complaints

Negative reviews on sizing or quality lead to proactive holds. Show return metrics and QA processes.

Business model drift

Operating differently than the onboarding questionnaire invites compliance reviews. Document every pivot.

High-ticket ratios

Large transactions (> $500) receive extra scrutiny. Include signed authorizations and invoices.
