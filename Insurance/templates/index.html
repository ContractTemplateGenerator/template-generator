California has some of the strongest consumer protection laws for insurance claims. The California Fair Claims Settlement Practices Regulations (Cal. Code Regs. tit. 10, Section 2695.1 et seq.) impose specific duties on insurers, including response deadlines, communication requirements, and prohibition of lowball tactics. Choose the Right Template
