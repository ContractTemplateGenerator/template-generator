CA California Auto Insurance Rules

California has specific regulations under CCR Title 10, Section 2695.10 that provide additional protections for auto insurance claimants:

  • Total Loss Valuations: Insurers must provide a written itemized valuation using comparable vehicles from your geographic area
  • Right to Choose Your Shop: Insurers cannot require you to use a specific repair shop
  • OEM Parts: Insurers must disclose if they want to use non-OEM (aftermarket) parts and get your consent
  • Rental Car Extensions: If repairs are delayed due to insurer issues, they must extend rental coverage
  • Minimum Coverage: California requires 15/30/5 liability coverage (15K per person, 30K per accident, 5K property damage)
