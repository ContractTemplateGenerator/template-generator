Protect your pitch deck and business plans when talking to potential investors

Important Note
Many VCs and institutional investors will not sign NDAs. This template works best for angel investors, family offices, and strategic investors.
Recommended Clauses
Confidential Info Exclusions Permitted Disclosures Term & Duration Non-Circumvention
Key Points for Investor NDAs
  • +
    Unilateral Protection: You share your pitch deck; they receive it.
  • +
    Non-Circumvention: Prevent them from going around you to your contacts.
  • +
    Residuals Clause: Investors may request this - understand the implications.
  • +
    Portfolio Company Exception: Some investors need to share with portfolio companies.
