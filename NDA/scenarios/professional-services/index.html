Why Professional Services NDAs Differ

Industry Compliance

Each profession has unique regulatory requirements that NDAs must address.

  • HIPAA for healthcare
  • SEC/FINRA for financial
  • State bar rules for legal
  • Industry-specific carve-outs

Client Relationships

Professional service NDAs often need mutual protection while preserving the service relationship.

  • Referral protection
  • Non-circumvention clauses
  • Client list confidentiality
  • Methodology protection

Work Product Rights

Balancing confidentiality with the need to showcase work and build portfolios.

  • Case study permissions
  • Portfolio carve-outs
  • Anonymized references
  • Testimonial rights
