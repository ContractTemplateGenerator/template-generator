Key Clauses for Business Deals

Understand the critical provisions in any business deal NDA

🔒 Definition of Confidential Information

Broad definitions protect more but may face enforceability challenges. Specific definitions are stronger but may miss some disclosures.

Negotiate Carefully

📅 Term and Survival

The NDA term (when you can share info) differs from survival period (how long it stays confidential). Trade secrets often need indefinite protection.

Key Decision Point

👥 Permitted Disclosures

Carve-outs for attorneys, accountants, and employees with need-to-know are standard. Watch for overly broad "affiliates" language.

Standard Clause

⚖ Non-Solicitation

Some NDAs include employee or customer non-solicitation. These go beyond confidentiality and require separate consideration.

Often Overreaching

📚 Residuals Clause

Allows using general knowledge retained in memory. Dangerous for true trade secrets; reasonable for non-technical discussions.

Watch Carefully

🏠 Governing Law & Venue

Determines where disputes are resolved. Home state is preferred; neutral venue is acceptable. Avoid agreeing to distant jurisdictions.

Practical Impact

Common Negotiation Points

Know what to push back on when reviewing an NDA

  • 1

    One-Way vs. Mutual

    If you'll share information too, insist on mutual obligations. One-way NDAs are only appropriate when one party is solely receiving.

  • 2

    Overly Broad Definitions

    Push back on "all information shared" language. Request specific categories or require written marking as "Confidential."

  • 3

    Unreasonable Duration

    5+ year terms for non-trade-secret information is excessive. 2-3 years is standard for most business discussions.

  • 4

    Injunctive Relief Without Limits

    While injunctions are standard, watch for clauses waiving your right to require bond posting or contest preliminary injunctions.

  • 5

    Burdensome Return/Destroy Obligations

    Ensure carve-outs for legally-required retention and automated backup systems that can't practically be purged.
