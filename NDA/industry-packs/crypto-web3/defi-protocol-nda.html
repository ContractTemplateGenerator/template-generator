🔒 Key Provisions for DeFi Protocol NDAs

  • TVL Strategy Protection
    Protect plans for attracting and retaining TVL, including planned incentive programs, liquidity mining designs, and partnerships with institutional depositors.
  • Yield Mechanism Design
    Classify yield generation strategies, fee structures, revenue sharing arrangements, and any proprietary mechanisms as confidential trade secrets.
  • Liquidity Arrangements
    Protect LP incentive terms, locked liquidity arrangements, market maker relationships, and any side agreements that affect protocol liquidity.
  • Oracle Configurations
    Protect oracle selection criteria, price feed configurations, and any custom oracle arrangements that could be exploited if disclosed.
  • Integration Terms
    Distinguish between public permissionless interfaces and confidential commercial integration terms including priority access, custom fee arrangements, or exclusive features.
  • MEV Protection
    Include provisions prohibiting exploitation of advance knowledge for MEV extraction, front-running, or sandwich attacks against the protocol or its users.

When to Use This NDA

Common DeFi scenarios requiring specialized protection

🤝 Protocol Partnerships

Protocol-to-protocol integrations, cross-protocol yield strategies, or collaborative liquidity arrangements.

💰 Liquidity Provider Terms

Custom arrangements with large LPs, locked liquidity terms, or incentive agreements beyond public programs.

🔍 Pre-Launch Features

Sharing upcoming features, mechanism changes, or roadmap items with potential integration partners.

🏢 Institutional Onboarding

Discussions with institutional depositors, market makers, or treasury management services.

⚠️ Common DeFi Protocol NDA Risks

Issue Risk Level Recommended Action
No MEV exploitation prohibition High Add explicit prohibition on front-running, sandwich attacks, or MEV extraction using disclosed info
Oracle configuration exposed without protection High Classify oracle arrangements as critical confidential information with extended protection
No distinction between public and private interfaces Medium Clarify that public smart contract ABIs are not confidential while commercial terms are
TVL strategy not explicitly protected Medium Enumerate TVL acquisition strategies, incentive programs, and LP terms as confidential
Standard term insufficient for trade secrets Low Add indefinite protection for proprietary yield mechanisms and algorithmic strategies

⚠️ DeFi Regulatory Considerations

DeFi protocols may be subject to securities, commodities, or money transmission regulations depending on their design and jurisdiction. NDAs for DeFi partnerships should include regulatory cooperation carve-outs for SEC, CFTC, FinCEN, and state regulators. Protocol teams should consult with regulatory counsel to understand how their specific mechanisms may be classified.
