Why Crypto Needs Specialized NDAs

Standard business NDAs miss critical Web3-specific concerns

⚖️ Regulatory Uncertainty

SEC, CFTC, and FinCEN have overlapping and evolving jurisdiction. NDAs must include broad carve-outs for regulatory cooperation and potential enforcement actions.

🌐 Pseudonymous Parties

Many crypto contributors operate pseudonymously. NDAs need wallet-based identification, on-chain attestations, and identity revelation provisions for breach scenarios.

🪙 Token Securities Risk

Premature token information disclosure can create securities law issues. NDAs should address insider trading prevention and material non-public information handling.

🔒 Smart Contract IP

Smart contract code and audit findings require special protection. NDAs must balance security transparency with proprietary methodology protection.

🏢 Decentralized Governance

DAO structures complicate traditional NDA enforcement. Provisions should address multi-sig requirements and community disclosure obligations.

📈 DeFi Protocol Risks

Protocol vulnerabilities and TVL strategies are high-value targets. NDAs need responsible disclosure frameworks and exploit prevention provisions.

These NDA templates are provided for informational purposes and are not a substitute for legal advice.
