Key SaaS NDA Provisions

What makes cloud NDAs different from standard agreements

  • 🔒

    Data Isolation and Segregation

    Specific provisions ensuring logical or physical separation of customer data in multi-tenant environments, with guarantees against cross-tenant data access.

  • 🔑

    API Credential and Key Management

    Protection requirements for API keys, OAuth tokens, and authentication credentials with specific handling, rotation, and revocation procedures.

  • 🌐

    Data Residency and Sovereignty

    Explicit requirements for data storage locations, restrictions on cross-border transfers, and compliance with local data protection laws.

  • 📊

    Uptime and Performance Metrics

    Confidentiality obligations for SLA performance data, incident reports, and system availability metrics that could affect competitive positioning.

  • 👥

    Subprocessor and Third-Party Access

    Requirements for notification, approval, and flow-down obligations when engaging subprocessors or third-party services with data access.

⚖️ Consult a Technology Attorney

Cloud and SaaS confidentiality requirements vary significantly based on data types, jurisdictions, and compliance frameworks. While our templates provide a strong foundation, we recommend having a technology attorney review any NDA involving sensitive data or enterprise deployments. Request a consultation.
