Why Government/Defense Needs Specialized NDAs

Standard business NDAs fail to address critical regulatory requirements

🚀 Export Control Compliance

ITAR and EAR impose strict controls on sharing defense articles and dual-use technology. Violations carry criminal penalties up to $1M and 20 years imprisonment.

🔒 Classification Requirements

Classified information requires special handling, storage, and transmission protocols. NDAs must address classification levels and derivative classification authority.

📄 CUI Handling Obligations

DFARS 252.204-7012 mandates specific cybersecurity controls and incident reporting for CUI. Non-compliance risks contract termination and debarment.

👥 Facility Clearance Requirements

NISPOM requires facility clearances for classified work. NDAs must verify FCL status and specify clearance level requirements.

📊 Government Audit Rights

FAR/DFARS provide extensive government audit rights. NDAs must include flow-down provisions and audit cooperation requirements.

⚖️ Procurement Integrity Act

The Procurement Integrity Act restricts disclosure of source selection and contractor bid information. Violations can result in criminal prosecution.

⚠️ Critical Legal Notice

Government and defense NDAs involve complex regulatory requirements including export controls, security classification, and federal acquisition regulations. Improper handling of controlled information can result in criminal prosecution, civil penalties, debarment, and loss of security clearances. These templates provide a foundation but must be reviewed by counsel experienced in government contracts and export control law before execution. The templates do not constitute legal advice and should not be used without appropriate legal review.
