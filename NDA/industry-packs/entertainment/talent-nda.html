Real-World Scenarios

How talent NDAs apply in common entertainment situations

🎭

Major Franchise Casting

An actor is cast in a superhero franchise. The NDA covers the role, costume designs, plot details, and even the existence of their involvement until the studio's announcement.

Tip: Ensure family exception allows telling spouse/partner under confidentiality.
📱

Influencer Brand Campaign

A social media influencer signs with a major brand for an unannounced product launch. The NDA restricts mentioning competitor products and requires pre-approval of all campaign-related content.

Tip: Negotiate clear guidelines on what constitutes "campaign-related" content.
🎤

Surprise Album Collaboration

A musician collaborates with a major artist on an unannounced track. The NDA prevents any mention of the collaboration, including in-studio photos or hints on social media.

Tip: Include provisions for credit attribution once the project is released.
🏆

Award Show Appearance

A presenter knows award winners in advance for rehearsals. The NDA includes severe penalties for spoiling results, including liquidated damages provisions.

Tip: Review liquidated damages amounts to ensure they're reasonable, not punitive.

Negotiation Tips for Talent

What to push back on when reviewing an NDA

  • 1

    Overbroad Social Media Restrictions

    Push back on restrictions that affect your ability to post unrelated content. NDAs should only restrict project-specific posts, not your entire social media presence.

  • 2

    Unlimited Duration

    Confidentiality obligations should end at some point (except for true trade secrets). Push for specific end dates tied to project release or a reasonable number of years.

  • 3

    Excessive Liquidated Damages

    Review any pre-set damage amounts. They should reflect actual potential harm, not serve as punishment. Amounts in the millions may be unenforceable.

  • 4

    Missing Publicity Carve-Outs

    Ensure the NDA explicitly permits approved promotional activities. Without carve-outs, you could technically breach by participating in official press junkets.

  • 5

    No Family Exception

    Request a limited exception to tell immediate family members (under their own confidentiality obligations) about your involvement in major projects.
