Union & Guild Compatibility

NDAs must work alongside industry guild requirements

SAG-AFTRA

Actor confidentiality must not prevent members from discussing working conditions with their union representatives.

DGA

Director agreements require specific provisions for creative control disputes that may involve confidential information.

WGA

Writer protections include arbitration rights for credit disputes that may require confidential script access.

IATSE

Crew confidentiality cannot prevent workers from reporting safety concerns to their union.

Teamsters

Transportation and location personnel often have access to sensitive logistics requiring specific protections.

PGA

Producer confidentiality agreements must align with Producers Guild best practices and credit determinations.

Real-World Production Scenarios

How production NDAs apply in common situations

🎥

Major Franchise Sequel

A production company is developing the next installment of a billion-dollar franchise. The NDA covers all aspects from script development to casting decisions, with code names for internal and external communications.

Tip: Include provisions for handling leaked information and rapid response protocols.
💰

International Co-Production

A US production company partners with European producers for an international co-production. The NDA must address different legal jurisdictions, tax incentive requirements, and cross-border information sharing.

Tip: Specify governing law and ensure enforceability in all relevant jurisdictions.
📺

Streaming Platform Pitch

A production company is pitching a series concept to multiple streaming platforms simultaneously. The NDA protects the concept while allowing pitches to competitors.

Tip: Include provisions clarifying that pitching the same concept to competitors is permitted.
🏆

Awards Campaign Coordination

Production companies coordinating on an awards campaign share screening schedules, voting strategy, and campaign budgets. The NDA prevents these details from reaching trade publications.

Tip: Set clear expiration dates tied to the awards cycle conclusion.
