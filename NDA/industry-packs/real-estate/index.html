When You Need a Real Estate NDA

Protect confidential information in these common scenarios

📎 Property Tours & Showings

Before showing properties to potential buyers, especially off-market listings with sensitive seller information.

📄 Due Diligence Access

When sharing rent rolls, environmental reports, title documents, and operating statements with prospective buyers.

🤝 Joint Venture Discussions

Before discussing partnership structures, capital contributions, profit splits, and exit strategies.

🏢 Development Planning

When sharing site plans, pro formas, anchor tenant negotiations, or land assemblage strategies.

💰 Investor Presentations

Before pitching fund opportunities, sharing track records, or discussing target acquisitions.

👤 Broker Cooperation

When sharing pocket listings with cooperating brokers or investor networks.

Key Clauses for Real Estate NDAs

Essential provisions for property transaction confidentiality

🔒 Definition of Confidential Information

Include property financials, tenant information, environmental reports, surveys, title documents, and pricing strategy.

Customize Carefully

🔗 Non-Circumvention

Prevents buyers from going around brokers to contact sellers directly, or partners from pursuing deals independently.

Critical for Brokers

📅 Term & Survival

Typically 2-3 years for acquisitions, longer for development deals. Consider deal timeline and information sensitivity.

Match to Deal Cycle

👥 Permitted Disclosures

Carve-outs for lenders, attorneys, accountants, and potential partners who need deal information.

Standard Provision

📚 Return of Materials

Require return or destruction of due diligence materials if deal doesn't close. Important for sensitive tenant data.

Important for Sellers

⚖ Specific Performance

Include injunctive relief provisions since monetary damages may be inadequate for real estate confidentiality breaches.

Recommended

🏛 State-Specific Considerations

California

Non-competes are void; focus on confidentiality and non-solicitation.

Texas

Strong trade secret protection; mineral rights may need special provisions.

New York

Consider cooperative apartment vs. condo disclosure requirements.

Florida

HOA disclosure rules; foreign investment reporting considerations.
