Critical: Patent Bar Date Protection

In biotech, premature disclosure can destroy patent rights worldwide. Our biotech NDAs include specific provisions for patent bar date protection, publication delays for patent filing, and clear invention ownership. Grace periods vary by jurisdiction (12 months US, none in Europe for most disclosures).

Essential Biotech NDA Provisions

Clauses that differentiate biotech NDAs from standard agreements

  • 💡

    Invention Disclosure Obligations

    Requirements to promptly disclose inventions arising from shared information, with clear ownership rules for joint developments.

  • 📚

    Publication Delay Rights

    Right to delay publication 60-90 days to file patent applications, protecting worldwide patentability in non-grace-period jurisdictions.

  • 🚫

    Non-Use for Competing Development

    Prohibition on using disclosed information to develop competing compounds or pursue the same therapeutic targets.

  • 📈

    Data Integrity Requirements

    Obligations to maintain data accuracy, prevent selective disclosure, and ensure research reproducibility.

  • 🔒

    Regulatory Filing Rights

    Clear delineation of who may reference shared data in regulatory submissions and under what conditions.
