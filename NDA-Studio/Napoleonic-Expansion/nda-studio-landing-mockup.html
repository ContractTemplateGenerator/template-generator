Their Clause (Residuals)
"Receiving Party may retain in the unaided memory of its personnel any Confidential Information and may use such retained information for any purpose..."
Your Response
Risk Level: HIGH

Why it's dangerous: This "residuals" clause lets them use anything they remember - including your trade secrets - for other clients.

Fallback: "No residual rights shall apply to trade secrets, source code, or customer data."

Email: "We're open to standard residuals for general business concepts, but we'd need to exclude trade secrets and proprietary code from this provision..."
