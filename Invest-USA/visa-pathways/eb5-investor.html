Investment Amounts: $800K TEA vs $1.05M Standard

â–¼

The EB-5 Reform and Integrity Act of 2022 established current investment thresholds based on project location.

$800,000 TEA Investment

  • Rural areas (population under 20,000)
  • High unemployment (150%+ national avg)
  • Infrastructure projects
  • Reserved visa allocation
  • Most regional center projects qualify

$1,050,000 Standard

  • Any commercial enterprise in US
  • Urban areas with normal employment
  • Greater project flexibility
  • Direct investment in own business
  • Same 10-job requirement
Investment must be "at risk" - no guaranteed returns or principal protection

Proving Lawful Source of Funds

â–¼
Source of funds is where most EB-5 cases face challenges

Acceptable Sources

  • Business profits, dividends, sale proceeds
  • Employment salary and bonuses
  • Real estate sale proceeds
  • Stock/investment liquidation
  • Gifts (with donor's SOW docs)
  • Inheritance
  • Secured loans (against personal assets)

Documentation Tips

  • Start gathering docs months early
  • Get notarized translations
  • Show complete paper trail
  • Avoid gaps in financial history
  • Tax returns match declarations
  • Bank statements (12-24 months)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get my investment back?

â–¼

Yes, but not until after your I-829 is approved - typically 5-7 years from investment. Capital must remain at risk during this period. Regional center investments have defined exit timelines; direct investments depend on business success.

What if the project fails?

â–¼

If the project fails to create required jobs, your I-829 may be denied and you could lose conditional residence. Due diligence on regional center track records is critical. Some investors obtain investment loss insurance.

Can I use borrowed money?

â–¼

Yes, if the loan is secured by your personal assets (not EB-5 project assets). You must prove collateral was lawfully obtained. Margin loans against stock portfolios and home equity loans are common.

Do I need to live near my investment?

â–¼

No. With your green card, you can live anywhere in the US. Regional center investments require no operational role. Direct investments need sufficient involvement but not physical presence at business location.

What are ongoing requirements?

â–¼

Keep investment deployed and ensure job creation targets are met. Cannot abandon US residence for extended periods. File I-829 within 90-day window before conditional card expires.
