Your Brand Ambassador Agreement

Why Use a Brand Ambassador Agreement?

A brand ambassador agreement protects both the company and the influencer by clearly defining expectations, compensation, and responsibilities. Key benefits include:

  • Clear deliverables - Specify exactly how many posts, stories, and what platforms
  • FTC compliance - Ensure proper disclosure of sponsored content
  • Compensation clarity - Define commissions, flat fees, or product gifting terms
  • Exclusivity protection - Prevent ambassadors from promoting competitors
  • Content rights - Establish who owns the content and how it can be used

What to Include in Your Agreement

Every brand ambassador agreement should address:

  1. Scope of services - Content requirements, platforms, and posting frequency
  2. Compensation structure - Commission rates, flat fees, payment timing
  3. Term and termination - How long the agreement lasts and how to end it
  4. Exclusivity - Whether the ambassador can work with competitors
  5. Content approval - Whether the brand must approve posts before publishing
  6. Intellectual property - Who owns the content created
