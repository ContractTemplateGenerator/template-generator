Thailand-Specific Considerations

Physical Presence Test Low Risk
Living in Thailand 340+ days annually easily satisfies the 330-day requirement for FEIE qualification.
Thai Tax Treaty Low Risk
US-Thailand tax treaty provides protection against double taxation and may offer additional foreign tax credit opportunities.
Recent Tax Changes Monitor
Thailand's new tax residency rules may affect US citizens. Stay informed about potential obligations on foreign-sourced income.
Banking & Compliance Manageable
Maintain US business banking for S-Corp operations. Thai banking regulations require proper structuring of account relationships.
