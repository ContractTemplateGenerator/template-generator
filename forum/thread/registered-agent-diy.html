FL_Attorney_M Attorney Sep 29, 2024

Main practical issue: registered agent must be available at a physical address during normal business hours. If you travel, work remotely from cafes, or just don't want to be tied down, you could miss service and end up with a default judgment against you.

If you're literally always home 9-5 M-F and don't mind your address being public, being your own agent is fine. Otherwise the $99/year is cheap peace of mind.
