WiseCounsel Attorney Nov 10, 2024

Red flag but not always a dealbreaker. Sometimes small business owners are just contract-averse, not malicious. Ask what specifically concerns them about your contract. Often they just don't want to involve lawyers or are intimidated by legal language.

Option: offer a simplified 1-page agreement that covers just the essentials: scope, payment terms, IP ownership.

Did a $10K project on "trust" once. Client disappeared after receiving deliverables. No contract = no recourse. Never again. At minimum get something in writing, even email confirmation of scope and payment terms.
