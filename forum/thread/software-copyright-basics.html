IP
IPLaw_Michelle Attorney Oct 20, 2024

You're right that copyright exists automatically when you write code. But registration gives you two huge benefits:

1. You can sue for infringement (can't file lawsuit without registration)
2. You can get statutory damages ($30K-150K per work) and attorney fees if registered BEFORE infringement

Registration costs $65 and takes 3-6 months. Worth doing for your core codebase.

Another reason to register: due diligence. If you ever sell your company or raise a proper Series A, investors will ask about IP ownership. Having registrations shows you took IP seriously. It's a "nice to have" that becomes "wish I had" later.
