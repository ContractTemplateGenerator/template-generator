Why Arizona formations are attractive

Low filing fees, easy online submissions, no publication requirement for corporations or nonprofits (most LLCs can avoid publication by using known counties), and a straightforward Department of Revenue portal for sales/payroll taxes.

Arizona LLC / PLLC step-by-step

  1. Name availability. Search ACC eCorp; reserve if desired.
  2. Statutory agent. Provide acceptance form signed by the agent (digital signature allowed).
  3. Articles of Organization. File online ($50 standard, $85 expedite). Include management structure, principal address, and agent info.
  4. Operating Agreement. I draft single-member or multi-member versions, including Series LLC language if needed.
  5. Publication. Required unless principal office is in Maricopa or Pima. For others, publish in an approved newspaper for three consecutive publications and file the affidavit.
  6. EIN. Filed with IRS; foreign owners use fax if no SSN.
  7. Arizona DOR & licensing. Register for Transaction Privilege Tax (sales tax), withholding, and unemployment tax via AZTaxes.gov.
  8. PLLCs/PCs. Obtain licensing board approval before filing; include required professional clauses.
Series LLC tip: Use distinct bank accounts and ledgers per series; I provide internal “Designation of Series” forms and instructions for ACC filings referencing the Series LLC structure.

Arizona corporations & nonprofits

Business corporations

File Articles (Form C010) online. Adopt bylaws, issue stock, and file annual reports. I handle EIN, Form 2553 for S-election, and franchise tax planning (Arizona has no corporate income tax filings beyond standard returns).

Benefit & professional corporations

Arizona allows Professional Corporations (PCs). I add statutory language, coordinate with licensing boards, and draft governance docs reflecting ownership/scope limits.

Nonprofit corporations

Articles of Incorporation for Nonprofit must include character of affairs and distribution clauses. After ACC filing, I prepare bylaws, conflict-of-interest policy, IRS Form 1023/1023EZ, and AZ tax exemption application.

Annual compliance

Corporations and nonprofits file annual reports (LLCs do not). I calendar deadlines and file for you so ACC doesn’t administratively dissolve the entity.

LPs, LLPs, foreign entities, and Series extras

Limited Partnerships (LP)

File Certificate of LP (Form L010) and designate a general partner (often an LLC). Publication is required unless the LP is maintained in Maricopa/Pima counties.

Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP)

Existing partnerships can register as LLPs to limit liability. File Statement of Qualification and renew every 5 years.

Foreign qualification

If you formed elsewhere, file Application for Registration with ACC, provide a Certificate of Good Standing, and publish if required. I also obtain your EIN if needed.

Statutory agent services

Arizona requires local service. I can act as statutory agent or coordinate with a trusted provider, and I prepare the acceptance to file with ACC.
