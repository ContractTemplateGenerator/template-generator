🎯 Recommended Checkbox Placement

  • ✓
    Separate from other consents: The TCPA consent checkbox should be distinct from other checkboxes (like terms acceptance or email signup).
  • ✓
    Above the submit button: Place the checkbox and disclosure where users will see it before submitting the form.
  • ✓
    Clear and conspicuous: Use readable font size (at least 12px), contrasting colors, and adequate spacing.
  • ✓
    Unchecked by default: The checkbox must be unchecked when the form loads. Pre-checked boxes are not valid consent.
  • ✓
    Optional consent: Do not require the checkbox to submit the form unless the communication is essential to the service.

⚠ Important Compliance Notes

This generator provides sample language only. TCPA compliance involves more than just disclosure text. You must also:

  • Maintain proper consent records with timestamps and evidence
  • Honor all opt-out requests within one business day
  • Scrub against the National Do Not Call Registry for marketing calls
  • Include sender identification in all messages
  • Avoid sending messages before 8am or after 9pm recipient's time
  • Use only approved short codes or 10DLC for high-volume SMS

Consult with a TCPA attorney to ensure your complete program is compliant.
