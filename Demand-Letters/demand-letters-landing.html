Know All Your Options

A demand letter isn't always the best path. Here are other remedies that may work better for your situation.

💳

Payment Disputes

Credit card chargebacks, PayPal disputes, and bank reversals often work faster than demand letters for purchase disputes.

Time limit: 60-120 days
Cost: Free
Learn about chargebacks →
🏢

Government Agencies

Consumer protection agencies, state attorneys general, and regulatory bodies can force businesses to act when individual demands fail.

Timeline: 2-6 months
Cost: Free
File a consumer complaint →
🏛

Small Claims Court

For amounts up to $10,000-$12,500 (varies by state), small claims offers an affordable, attorney-free way to get a binding judgment.

Timeline: 1-3 months
Cost: $75-200
Small claims guide →
☆

Online Reviews

Strategic, factual reviews on BBB, Yelp, Google, and industry platforms create reputational pressure that motivates resolution.

Timeline: Immediate
Cost: Free
Review strategy tips →
💬

Platform Disputes

Marketplace platforms like Amazon, eBay, Upwork, and Fiverr have built-in resolution processes that can force refunds.

Timeline: 1-4 weeks
Cost: Free
Platform dispute guides →
📝

Formal Demand Letter

A professionally drafted demand letter on law firm letterhead signals serious intent and often triggers settlement without litigation.

Timeline: 2-4 weeks
Cost: $240-450
