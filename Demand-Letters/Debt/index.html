Debt Collection Demand Letters

Templates and guides for responding to debt collectors, disputing credit report errors, validating debts under the FDCPA, and negotiating settlements with creditors.

💰 Available Guides

Bank Fee & Account Dispute Letters →

Templates for disputing unauthorized bank fees, account errors, and requesting fee reversals from financial institutions.

Credit Report Error Demand Letters (FCRA) →

Dispute inaccurate information on your credit report using Fair Credit Reporting Act protections and demand corrections from credit bureaus.

Debt Collector Harassment Letters (FDCPA/Rosenthal) →

Stop harassing debt collection practices using federal FDCPA and California Rosenthal Act protections.

Debt Validation & Dispute Letters (FDCPA) →

Request debt validation within 30 days of initial contact to verify the debt is legitimate and the amount is accurate.

Lump Sum Settlement Offer Letters →

Negotiate reduced payoff amounts with creditors by offering lump sum settlements in exchange for debt forgiveness.

Medical Debt Collection Demand Letters →

Challenge medical billing errors, negotiate hospital bills, and dispute medical debt collection with itemized statement requests.

Mortgage Servicer Error Demand Letters →

Dispute mortgage payment application errors, escrow miscalculations, and demand corrections under RESPA.

Payment Plan Proposal Letters →

Propose structured payment arrangements with creditors to avoid collection actions and manage debt repayment.

Personal Guarantor Demand Letters →

Respond to personal guarantee enforcement or demand payment from guarantors on defaulted business obligations.

Pre-Suit Demand for Past Due Accounts →

Formal pre-litigation demand letters for collecting past due accounts before initiating legal action.

Promissory Note Default Demand Letters →

Demand payment on defaulted promissory notes with proper notice of default and acceleration of remaining balance.
