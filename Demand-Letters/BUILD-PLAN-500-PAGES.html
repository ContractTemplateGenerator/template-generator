How They Connect: Pillar pages → embed generator iframe → generator handles the actual letter creation. Pillar = SEO content, Generator = tool.
Benefit: Generator handles all 6 states automatically. Build CA pillar pages first; other states get generator functionality immediately without separate pages until Phase 6.
PAGE TYPES: Old vs New Approach

OLD: Simple Generator Pages
Just a form that generates a letter. Minimal content, poor SEO value. Example: a page with just "Fill in your details → Get letter"

NEW: Comprehensive Pillar Guide Pages
Full educational experience with 7 tabs of unique content. The generator is ONE tab (embedded via iframe). User gets:
• Overview — What this claim is, key stats, when to use
• Legal Basis — State statutes, case law citations, elements to prove
• Checklist — Evidence to gather, interactive checkboxes
• Damages — How to calculate what you're owed, sample calculations
• Sample Language — Copy-paste paragraphs for DIY letters
• Generator — Embedded tool (iframe to shared generator engine)
• Next Steps — What to do if they pay/don't pay, resources

WHY THIS IS BETTER FOR SEO:
• More content = more keywords = better rankings for related queries
• User spends more time on page (engagement signal)
• Answers questions Google users are asking ("how do I calculate waiting time penalties?")
• Natural internal linking opportunities
• Each pillar page can rank for 10+ related keywords instead of just 1

⚠️ INTERPRETING THE PHASES BELOW

All "pages" listed in Phases 1-4 should be built as Pillar Guide Pages (7-tab format with embedded generator), NOT simple generator pages.

PRODUCTION URL STRUCTURE (NOT WORKBENCH!):
Pages must go directly to production paths for Google to index them:
/Demand-Letters/[Category]/[situation-name].html

Examples:
• /Demand-Letters/Insurance/bad-faith-claim-denial.html
• /Demand-Letters/Employment/unpaid-overtime-wages.html
• /Demand-Letters/PI/rear-end-collision.html

CA-FIRST STRATEGY (Low Risk, High Authority):
Build CALIFORNIA-ONLY pillar pages first. Avoids Google duplicate content flags for a new domain.

Why CA-Only First:
• You're a CA-licensed attorney = strongest moat, highest E-E-A-T
• New domains need trust - general + state pages look like thin content farming
• CA pages can rank for BOTH "california X demand letter" AND "X demand letter"
• Generator handles all 6 states automatically via URL params - no separate pages needed yet

Per situation, you build:
• 1 situation module in /generator/ (form fields + letter template + statutes for ALL 6 states)
• 1 California pillar page (CA statutes, deadlines, case law, penalties)
= 1 high-authority CA page per situation

PHASE 6+ (FUTURE): General/all-states pages after CA pages are indexed and ranking.

SAFE PARALLEL AGENT BUILD ORDER:
Agents editing the same file will conflict! Use this safe pattern:

Step 1: ONE agent adds situation modules to generator (sequential)
Step 2: Wait for Step 1 to complete
Step 3: Launch parallel agents for CA pillar pages (each agent = different file)

Example for 10 situations:
• Agent 0: Add 10 modules to generator/index.html (one agent, sequential)
• Agents 1-10: Build 10 CA pillar pages (parallel, each creates different file)
= 10 production pages from one batch
