Risk Spotlight

Security Classification
  • Tokens with dividend or voting rights often trigger SEC registration.
  • Action: Consider pure utility or governance-only structures.
Lock-Up Enforcement
  • Smart contract or escrow is required to enforce post-token lock-ups.
  • Action: Sync generator lock-up term with on-chain logic or custody service.
Standard Vesting
  • 4-year schedules with 1-year cliff are industry norm.
  • Action: Use generator defaults, adjust only if special milestone triggers apply.
Bankable Pro Tips
  • Automate Doc Tracking: Store each export with a unique version stamp for audit trails.
  • Two-Way Edits: If counterparty requests changes, update generator inputs then re-export—no manual merge.
  • Jurisdiction Match: Set governing law to where your entity is formed to avoid forum-shopping disputes.
  • Escrow Alignment: If using escrow for vesting/lock-up, mirror escrow release schedule precisely in generator.
